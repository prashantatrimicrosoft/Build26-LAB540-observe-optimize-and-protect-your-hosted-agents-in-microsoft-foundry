## Rental Policies

- All prices are in USD per day.
- Minimum rental age is 21 (25 for luxury vehicles). Young driver surcharge may apply for ages 21–24.
- A valid driver's license is required; International Driving Permit (IDP) required in Japan and recommended elsewhere.
- Full insurance (collision, theft, liability) is available for an additional $15–25/day.
- Fuel policy is full-to-full: pick up with a full tank, return with a full tank.
- GPS units are included at no additional charge for all vehicles.
- One-way rentals within the same city are permitted at no extra cost.
- Cross-border rentals are NOT permitted (e.g., you cannot drive a Paris rental to Germany).
