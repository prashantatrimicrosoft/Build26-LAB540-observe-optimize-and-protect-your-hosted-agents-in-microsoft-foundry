## Booking Policies

- All prices are in USD and are per person, one-way.
- Seats are subject to availability and may change without notice.
- Business and First Class include complimentary lounge access, priority boarding, and premium meals.
- Economy class includes one carry-on bag and one personal item.
- Changes and cancellations are subject to a fee depending on the fare class.
- Children under 2 fly free on a parent's lap; ages 2–11 receive a 25% discount on Economy fares.
